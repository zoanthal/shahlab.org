## Mobile Health & Clinical Decision Support

We develop mobile applications and clinical decision support tools that serve both clinical and research functions. Several of these tools are used by clinicians worldwide.

### Anesthesiologist App

The Anesthesiologist app is a comprehensive quick reference and calculator for anesthesia providers. It includes drug dosing calculators, pediatric emergency references, fasting guidelines, airway algorithms, and other clinical decision support features. The app has been downloaded over 500,000 times on Android and iOS and is used in nearly every country. Beyond its clinical utility, the app serves as a platform for embedded research, including in-app surveys that have generated datasets on global anesthesia practice patterns.

### SUGARx

SUGARx is a perioperative hyperglycemia management app for iOS and Android. It implements institutional insulin management protocols and provides real-time dosing guidance for perioperative glucose control.

### Survalytics

Survalytics is an open-source, cloud-integrated experience sampling, survey, and analytics module for Android apps. It enables researchers to embed validated survey instruments within mobile applications and collect longitudinal data with minimal user burden. Survalytics was published in *JMIR mHealth and uHealth* and is freely available for integration into other research applications.

### ANTICIPATE

ANTICIPATE is a mobile reference for pediatric syndromes relevant to anesthesiologists. It provides syndrome-specific anesthetic considerations, airway management guidance, and associated conditions for rare pediatric syndromes encountered in the operating room.

### PHI Scrubber

PHI Scrubber is a tool for removing protected health information from research datasets. It handles a wide range of file formats encountered in clinical research, including EEG and neuroimaging files (EDF, FIF, SET, BrainVision), NIfTI neuroimaging volumes, MATLAB files, images, and DOCX documents.

### UW Anesthesia Education App

The UW Anesthesia Education App is a full-stack education platform for anesthesia trainees and attendings at the University of Washington. It supports case logging, didactic scheduling, evaluation management, and educational content delivery.

### Research on Mobile Health Adoption

Beyond tool development, we study how clinicians adopt and use mobile health technologies. This research examines download patterns, usage demographics, feature engagement, and the effectiveness of in-app survey methodology for clinical research. Our work on global health informatics uses mobile app data to understand anesthesia practice variation across countries and resource settings.
